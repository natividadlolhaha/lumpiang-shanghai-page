Lumpiang Shanghai
Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 cup carrots, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Combine the ground pork, carrots, onions, green onions, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place a small amount of filling on a spring roll wrapper and roll tightly, sealing the edges with water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Drain excess oil and enjoy with dipping sauce.
